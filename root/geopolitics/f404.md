# F404-IN20 Tracker

The General Electric F404-IN20 is a high bypass turbofan jet engine used in the LCA Mk1A series of fighter jets. The delays in the delivery of this jet by G.E. has caused significant delays in the LCA programme, thus slowing down the fleet modernization of the Indian Air Force.
